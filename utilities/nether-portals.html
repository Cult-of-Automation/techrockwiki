Ghost Portals

Ghost portals are the result of a nether portal being saved within the world files, but then crashing the world, which retains the portal within the files, even after the corresponding portal has been manually removed. They allow entities to appear in mid-air. They are made by mining a portal, waiting 15 seconds and then crashing the game.

Discovered by: Flolan
