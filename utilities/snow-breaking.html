Allowing any given block affected by gravity to fall onto an upwards facing piston (can be sticky or normal) with a non-full block with a height less than a bottom slab, the gravity blocks will not break and turn into an item, but instead remain in falling block form, but stationary.

Stacking snow layers onto a retracted piston head in large enough quantities will delete blocks above it, given that enough snow layers are present.
