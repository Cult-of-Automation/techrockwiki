Random shuffling between chunks

  • Every 20gt (1 sec)
  • Chunk-wise tile entity list shuffled
    • Every 1gt (1/20 sec)
    • Causes piston order within a chunk to be unpredictable
  • Consumer components within a chunk are updated in a predictable order
    • Depends on the order in which these components were placed
    • Depends on the number of consumers in the circuit
    • Depends on whether the producer is in the same chunk as the consumers
    • Depends on nothing else, therefore not directional or locational
