Wither cages are a containment area for the wither boss, which are constructed to perform tasks that would usually be automated using TNT. Examples include: 

  • Automatic Tree Farms
  • Concrete Converters
  • Cobblestone Generators
    In Bedrock Edition, the only reliable wither cages are made out of the bedrock block, which is contrary to popular belief, obtainable in survival. Wither cages require a dispenser shooting the wither (usually fed by a snow golem generating snow balls being broken by a piston), which will break blocks in a 4x4x4 box centered on the wither (verify).
